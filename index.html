WelCome Feni Computer Institute

Feni Computer Institute is a technology based institution in Feni Sadar Upazila, Bangladesh. [1] It is directed under The Directorate of Technical Education(DTE). This is the first government ICT based polytechnic institute for diploma in engineering courses in Bangladesh. It provides diploma in engineering program in three technologies, they are 1. Data Telecommunication and Networking (DNT), 2. Computer Science and Technology (CST) and 3. Telecommunication Technology.
